xrp-ledger.toml Checker

If you run an XRP Ledger validator or use the XRP Ledger for your business, you can provide information about your usage of the XRP Ledger to the world in a machine-readable xrp-ledger.toml file.

This tool allows you to verify that your xrp-ledger.toml file is syntactically correct and deployed properly.
